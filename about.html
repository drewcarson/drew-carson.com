Who

Inspired by the playful approach of art I am a Barcelona based spatial designer interested in the dialogue between fabrication and artisanal craftsmanship, while integrating forward thinking technology. Exploring towards a more efficient tomorrow with the utilization of robotics and digital fabrication techniques, I am interested in blurring the barriers between mediums, in hopes to provide a harmonious creative design.

-Drew Carson
