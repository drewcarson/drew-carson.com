PERCEBES


While working for VIMOD Studio


"The Lift" is a new electronic music club in Mantova, Italy. The people behind it enjoy the electronic ungerground culture as much as we do, so we had a great opportunity to field crazy creations. To increase the ephemoral experience we decided to rethink the space and give it some weird musically derived lighting. We built this amazing geometrical wood structure filled with more than 1000 LEDs.


The core idea driving the project was to create an organic system of dancing particles in the light cells. Through som musical programming algorithms the particles move, grow, and fade following the frequencies and beats emerging from the DJ set. We had a great opening with "Floating Points (London)" who brought our particle system to the limits. →
